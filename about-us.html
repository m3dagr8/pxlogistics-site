About PX Logistics

Reliable Midwest transportation built on trust, efficiency, and consistency.

PX Logistics is a Midwest-based transportation company focused on delivering reliable and efficient freight solutions for businesses of all sizes. We specialize in dependable regional logistics backed by professional drivers, modern equipment, and customer-focused service.

Our goal is simple — move freight safely, on time, and with complete transparency. Whether supporting local deliveries or long-haul operations, we work closely with our partners to ensure smooth and consistent operations.

What We Do

  • Local Midwest Transportation — Fast and dependable regional delivery.
  • Power Only Services — Reliable tractor support for customer trailers.
  • Dry Van Freight — Secure transportation for general cargo.
  • Refrigerated Shipping — Temperature-controlled transport for sensitive goods.

Our Commitment

At PX Logistics, we prioritize safety, communication, and reliability. We believe strong partnerships and consistent service are the foundation of successful logistics operations.
